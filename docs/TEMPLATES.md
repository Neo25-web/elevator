Agar 2 minute nikaal kar hamare kaam ke baare mein Google pe review likh dein to bari meharbani hogi: [REVIEW_LINK]. Shukriya!

**Tips:**
- Send individually, not in a broadcast list — personal messages get far more responses
- 5–10 per week is enough; a sudden flood of reviews in one day looks suspicious to Google
- If a client replies but doesn't post, politely follow up once after a few days
